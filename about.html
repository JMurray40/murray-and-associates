About Murray & Associates

We're more than bookkeepers — we're trusted financial partners for small business owners who want clarity, confidence, and control over their finances.

Our Story

Built to Serve Small Business

Murray & Associates was founded with a clear purpose: to give small business owners the financial support and clarity they deserve, without the cost and complexity of a full-time accounting department.

We've worked with entrepreneurs, freelancers, retailers, service professionals, and nonprofits throughout the Washington DC area and across the country. No matter the industry or size, we bring the same commitment to accuracy, transparency, and approachable expertise.

Many of our clients came to us overwhelmed by messy books, behind on their records, or simply unsure of whether their finances were being handled correctly. We love taking that burden off their shoulders — and watching them thrive as a result.
