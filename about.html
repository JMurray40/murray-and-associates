Our Story

Meet Jason Murray, Founder of Murray & Associates

Jason Murray is a University of Notre Dame-trained accountant with over two decades of professional experience. His career began in public accounting in 2003, including four years in audit — two of them at Grant Thornton, one of the world's leading multinational accounting firms — where he developed a rigorous foundation in financial controls, compliance, and reporting standards.

After his time in audit, Jason built an extensive career in accounting consulting, partnering with top staffing firms including Robert Half and KForce to serve clients across industries. That experience exposed him to hundreds of business environments and gave him a rare depth of perspective on what separates businesses with healthy finances from those constantly playing catch-up.

Murray & Associates was founded to bring that same big-firm expertise directly to small business owners — without the big-firm price tag or the impersonal service. Jason believes every business, regardless of size, deserves accurate books, clear financial insight, and a trusted advisor they can actually call.
